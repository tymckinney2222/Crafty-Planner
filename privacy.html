Privacy Policy for Crafty Planner

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

1. Overview

Crafty Planner, developed by Tyriah App Studios, is designed to help makers manage their business data. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about how your data is handled.

2. Data Collection & Storage

Local Data: By default, your business data (orders, expenses, inventory) is stored locally on your device using browser-based storage. This data remains under your control.

Account Data: If you choose to use our Cloud Sync features, we collect your email address via Google OAuth to create and manage your account. This is necessary to sync your data across multiple devices.

Financial Data: Any financial information you enter (revenue, supply costs) is used solely for the app's internal calculators and reports and is never shared with third parties.

3. Data Deletion (The Right to be Forgotten)

We provide two ways to manage and delete your data:

  • Local Deletion: You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser cache or uninstalling the application.
  • Account Deletion: If you have created a cloud account, you can request full deletion of your account and all associated cloud data by clicking the "Delete Account" button in the app settings or by emailing us directly at tyriahappstudios@gmail.com. All data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days of the request.

4. Third-Party Services

Crafty Planner is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect technical information (device model, OS version) to provide app updates and crash analytics. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for further details.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at: tyriahappstudios@gmail.com
