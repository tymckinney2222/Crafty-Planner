Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

The short version: Your business data stays on your device by default. Nothing is shared or sold. If you choose to turn on Cloud Sync or scan receipts with AI, some data is sent to trusted services (Google, Anthropic) so those features can work. You can delete your data anytime.

This page explains the details — in plain English.

Crafty Planner ("the App," "we," "us") is built by Tyriah App Studios. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Crafty Planner, you agree to the practices described here.

1. What We Collect

Business data you enter

Crafty Planner stores the information you type into the app — customer names, orders, items, prices, expenses, recipes, inventory, notes, and related business data. By default, this is saved locally on your device in browser storage and is not transmitted anywhere.

Account information (only if you use Cloud Sync)

Cloud Sync is optional and only activates if you sign in with your Google account. If you do, we collect:

  • Your Google account email address
  • Your display name and profile picture (from your Google account)
  • A unique Google-issued user identifier

This lets us store your business data in the cloud so you can access it from multiple devices.

Subscription information (only if you upgrade to Pro)

Crafty Planner Pro is sold as a subscription through the Google Play Store. When you subscribe, Google handles the payment. We receive a subscription status ("active" or "inactive") to unlock Pro features. We never see or store your payment card information.

Receipt images (only if you use AI Receipt Scanner)

If you take a photo of a receipt to auto-fill an expense, that image is temporarily sent to Anthropic's Claude API (via our secure server) so the AI can extract the store, date, items, and amounts. We do not store the image on our servers after processing. See section 3 for details.

Technical information

The Google Play Store may automatically collect technical information about your device (model, operating system version, crash reports) to support app distribution and quality. This is handled by Google, not by us. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the limited data we collect for a small number of clearly-defined purposes:

  • To run the app's features — storing your orders, showing your dashboard, generating invoices, etc.
  • To sync your data across devices — only if you've enabled Cloud Sync
  • To verify your subscription — to unlock Pro features for paid users
  • To process receipt images — only when you tap "Scan Receipt" and choose a photo
  • To respond to support requests — if you email us

What we never do: We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not use your business data to train AI models. We do not profile you or track you across other apps or websites.

3. Third-Party Services We Use

Crafty Planner relies on a few outside services to make cloud and AI features possible. Each one is used for a specific purpose, and each has its own privacy policy:

Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore)

Used to sign you in with your Google account and store your business data in the cloud when you enable Cloud Sync. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest on Google's servers. See Firebase Privacy and Security.

Google Play Billing

Used to process Pro subscription purchases. All payment processing is handled by Google — we do not see or store card numbers, bank details, or billing addresses. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Anthropic (Claude API)

Used only when you tap "Scan Receipt" to auto-extract expense details from a photo. The image is sent via our secure Cloudflare Worker to Anthropic's Claude API, which returns the extracted text. Anthropic's handling of API data is governed by their Privacy Policy. Anthropic states that API inputs are not used to train their models by default.

Cloudflare (Worker infrastructure)

We use Cloudflare Workers to securely route receipt-scanner and billing-verification requests to the services above. Cloudflare may log technical request metadata (IP address, timestamp) as part of its normal network operations. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

Local data is stored in your browser/device storage and never leaves your device unless you enable Cloud Sync.

Cloud data (if you enable it) is stored on Google Firebase servers. Firebase encrypts your data both in transit (TLS) and at rest, and access is restricted to your authenticated account.

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no system is perfectly secure. If we become aware of a security incident that affects your data, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by applicable law.

5. Data Retention

Local data stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

Cloud data is retained while your account is active. If you request deletion (see section 6), all associated cloud data is permanently removed within 30 days.

Subscription records may be retained by Google Play for their own legal and tax purposes, separately from our app. You can manage or cancel subscriptions anytime in the Google Play Store.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to access, export, correct, and delete your data at any time.

View and export your data

Crafty Planner includes built-in export tools for orders, customers, expenses, and full JSON backups (in Tools → Backup). Use these to download a copy of your data anytime.

Delete local data

You can delete all local app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage from your device's settings.

Delete cloud data

If you've enabled Cloud Sync, you can request full deletion of your cloud account and all associated data by emailing us at tyriahappstudios@gmail.com. We will delete all cloud-stored data within 30 days.

Cancel your subscription

Pro subscriptions can be cancelled anytime through the Google Play Store. Cancellation stops future billing; Pro features remain available until the end of the current billing period.

7. Children's Privacy

Crafty Planner is intended for adults running small or hobby businesses. The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. International Users

Crafty Planner is operated from the United States. If you use the App from outside the U.S., your data may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers (listed in section 3) operate. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the App evolves or as required by law. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will also be announced in the app. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
