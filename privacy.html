Privacy Policy for Crafty Planner

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

1. Overview

Crafty Planner is a local-first business dashboard. We believe your business data should remain private and under your control.

2. Data Collection & Storage

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All information you enter—including orders, expenses, and inventory—is stored locally on your device using your browser's internal storage.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect certain technical information (such as device model, OS version, and crash reports) to provide app updates and analytics. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.

4. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all your information at any time by clearing your browser cache or uninstalling the application.
